Meet the Innovator

Mr. Richard Nsor

A male entrepreneur from Bosomkyekye, Ashanti Region is addressing environmental pollution caused by plastic waste by creating petrol and diesel from waste materials like plastic bottles and bags. His solution aims to reduce pollution while providing a healthier environment

Innovation

Application of scientific knowledge that converts plastic waste into high-quality fuel through an energy-efficient process i.e pyrolysis

Recycling

Transforming problematic waste streams into valuable resources, creating a closed-loop system that reduces environmental impact.

Eco-Friendly

Protecting our planet by removing plastic waste from ecosystems

Our Services

Collecting and Sorting Plastic Waste

We begin by collecting plastic waste from urban, industrial, and marine sources. This waste is then meticulously sorted to ensure only recyclable plastics are used in the process.

Recycling Plastic into Fuel

Our innovative technology breaks down plastic waste into its fundamental components, converting it into clean, usable energy sources like diesel and petrol. This process is efficient, eco-friendly, and reduces landfill waste.

Producing and Distributing Clean Fuel

The final product is high-quality diesel and petrol, ready to power vehicles and machinery. We ensure the fuel meets environmental standards and is distributed to industries and consumers responsibly.
